Pratik Kalshetti

Bio

Pratik is a MTech+PhD dual degree student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. His PhD thesis is on Hand Avatar Reconstruction and Tracking, advised by Parag Chaudhuri. He has worked on hand pose estimation, mesh-based 3D hand tracking, personalized hand shape modeling and hand avatar reconstruction. His broad research interests are computer graphics and 3D computer vision. He was an intern at Adobe Research under the mentorship of Thibault Groueix and Vova Kim, where he worked in collaboration with Alec Jacobson and Oliver Wang. Previously, he completed his BTech in Computer Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur.

Pratik was featured at SIGGRAPH 2023 for becoming one of the eight students ever to have been placed in top three in the ACM Student Research Competition, twice (2019, 2022). He served as a student volunteer at SIGGRAPH 2018 and SIGGRAPH 2021. He won the Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship India 2017 and was also awarded TCS Research Scholar Fellowship in 2019.
