About

My PhD thesis is on Hand Avatar Reconstruction and Tracking, advised by Parag Chaudhuri. Specifically, I have worked on hand pose estimation, mesh-based 3D hand tracking, personalized hand shape modeling and hand avatar reconstruction. My broad research interests are computer graphics and 3D computer vision. I was an intern at Adobe Research under the mentorship of Thibault Groueix and Vova Kim, where I worked in collaboration with Alec Jacobson and Oliver Wang. Previously, I did my BTech in Computer Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur.
